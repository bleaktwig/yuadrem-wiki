# Pagram
Pagram is the god of Yellow, order, and civilization. His manifestation is stone, and is celebrated in the formation of cities and their associated rituals.

Pagram's representation is a swallow-tailed hummingbird, his fluttering following the flowers that decorate a city. As a [hume](../kins/hume.md), he is commonly depicted as a horned philosopher wearing white clothing, pompously adorned in gold.
